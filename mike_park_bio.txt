Mike Park obtained his bachelor's degree at the University of Southern
California. He attended the George Washington University and NASA
Langley Joint Institute for the Advancement of Flight Sciences for his
master's degree. He earned a Ph.D. in Computation Fluid Mechanics from
the Massachusetts Institute of Technology and is currently employed by
the NASA Langley Research Center as a FUN3D developer in the
Computational AeroSciences Branch. His research interests include
adjoint methods for grid adaptation and sonic boom prediction.
